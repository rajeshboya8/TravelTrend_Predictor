How to Use TravelTrend Predictor (TTP)

  1. Open the homepage and click Get Started.
  2. Fill the prediction form with:
    • Year, Duration of stay
    • Spends ($), Travel Mode, Purpose
    • Quarter and Market/Country
  3. Click Predict Tourists to get the expected number of visitors.
  4. Use the result to plan resources, pricing, or marketing campaigns.

History

Recent predictions appear after each prediction on the result page.

  • Make a prediction to populate the history.
  • Refresh the prediction page to view the latest entry.
