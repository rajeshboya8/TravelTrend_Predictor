TravelTrend Predictor (TTP)

TravelTrend Predictor (TTP) is a Django web application that forecasts expected tourist visits using a trained XGBoost regression model. It considers inputs such as quarter, travel mode, purpose, year, duration of stay, market segment, and spending to produce accurate predictions that can support planning, marketing, and operations.

The app features a simple prediction form, responsive UI with Materialize CSS, and fast inference from a pre-trained model. It is designed for clarity and ease of use.
